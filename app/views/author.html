The first book that I read from cover to cover was The Mystery of the Burnt Cottage by Enid Blyton. I was probably six, didn’t understand half of it, but saw it through; I am pretty sure my love affair with reading began then. Like any other affair, it saw its ups and downs. I’ve read some wonderful literature and also utter tripe.

Writing was a natural progression and a long cherished dream. For whatever my writing is worth, I am glad I wrote; gladder still that I continue to write. I am now home!
